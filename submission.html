July 20, 2017

August 19, 2017

Camera-ready deadline:
August 25, 2017

Workshop date:
October 23, 2017 (AM only)

The manuscripts should be submitted as PDF files and should be no more than 9 pages (shorter submissions welcome, think of the reviewers!), and formatted according to the ICCV 2017 author guidelines. Each paper will be peer-reviewed by at least two reviewers. Acceptance will be based on relevance to the workshop, novelty, and technical quality. In submitting a manuscript to this workshop, the authors acknowledge that no paper substantially similar in content has been submitted to another workshop or conference during the review period.
